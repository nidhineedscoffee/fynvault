## Use cases to show

1. Cash flow: `Explain cash-flow pressure and runway using verified records.`
2. Receivables: `Which invoices are overdue?`
3. Payables: `Which vendor bill is oldest unpaid?`
4. GST: `Why does April GST not match sales?`
5. TDS: `What TDS compliance gaps are still open?`
6. Payroll: `Did payroll increase in June?`
7. Contracts and renewals: `Which contract needs renewal?`
8. Validation: `What is blocking Intelligence Ready?`
9. Financial memory: `Who are our top customers this quarter?`
10. Advisory: `What advisory opportunities do you see from verified records?`
11. Reports and exports: `Generate a GST CSV from verified records.` and `Generate an MIS workbook in Excel.`

## What to point out during the demo

- Processing stages: collection, classification, extraction, validation, normalization, memory build, and Intelligence Ready.
- Validation issues: GST mismatch, duplicate invoice, missing bank reference, vendor normalization, TDS and missing-input issues.
- Financial memory: customer concentration, overdue receivables, overdue payables, renewal due, and payroll anomaly.
- Output discipline: Ask Fynny explains verified evidence and does not invent financial facts.

## Demo success criteria

- Gmail sync brings in client-specific finance attachments cleanly.
- The Aster Foods pack populates enough evidence to show all major product surfaces.
- Ask Fynny answers each prompt from validated data.
- The team can download GST and MIS outputs in the requested format.
- The CA can explain product value through one client story instead of jumping between generic examples.
